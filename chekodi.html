Chekodi Food App

  • JAVA
  • Hibernate
  • Spring
  • JSP
  • MySQL
  • HTML and CSS

"Chekodi" is an innovative food delivery app designed to redefine the way people experience dining from the comfort of their homes. Inspired by the convenience and efficiency of modern platforms like Swiggy, Chekodi connects users with a diverse range of restaurants and eateries, offering an extensive menu of cuisines to satisfy every craving.

With its user-friendly interface, real-time order tracking, and secure payment options, Chekodi ensures a seamless and enjoyable food delivery experience. The app also focuses on delivering meals fresh and hot, with an emphasis on timely service and customer satisfaction. Whether it's a quick snack, a hearty meal, or a gourmet treat, Chekodi is your trusted partner for delicious food delivered right to your doorstep.
